The CX-70 PHEV: Electrified Efficiency

The Mazda CX-70 PHEV is our newest Plug-In Hybrid Electric Vehicle. It’s got everything you’d expect from an agile 2-row SUV, plus standard i-Activ AWD®, a generous interior, and an efficient and electrified powertrain.

The CX-90 PHEV: Flexibility & Agility

Plug-In Hybrid Electric Vehicles like the CX-90 run all-electric for shorter trips or on a hybrid system of combustion and electric for longer journeys. The surprisingly nimble CX-90 PHEV was the first Mazda 3-row SUV to offer an all-electric driving range of up to 26 miles.
